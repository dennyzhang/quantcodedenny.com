Personal Top Priority

dennyzhang

November 3, 2025

URL: https://quantcodedenny.com/posts/personal-top-priority/

Key Objectives - FCM

  1. Family: Maintain a healthy and happy family

    • Build strong connection with kids
    • Nurture relationship with spouse
    • Maintain physical health and manage stress

  2. Confidence: Strengthen confidence and growth

    • Increase market value and professional impact
    • Improve appearance and communication skills
    • Deepen meaningful connections

  3. Money: Grow wealth effectively

    • Earn through smart, sustainable investing
    • Focus energy on high-leverage opportunities

Guiding Principles

  1. Identify and protect priorities
    • Know what to do and what not to do
    • Bias toward problem solving, not perfection
  2. Improve mindset
    • Apply growth mindset and first principles
  3. Spend smartly to earn more
    • Pay for services that save time and energy
    • Avoid over-optimizing trivial details

Build Good Habits & Stop Bad Habits

Good Habits

  1. Practice thoughtful design reviews
    • Gather and share impactful stories at work
    • Say no to local or low-impact optimizations
  2. Write weekly summaries to reflect and refocus

Bad Habits

  • Stop setting too many small goals
  • Stop struggling in dead ends
  • Avoid overthinking without action

Clear Vision

  1. Stay deeply connected with kid and spouse
  2. Remain energetic and positive after work
  3. Deprioritize distractions and stay focused on what matters most
